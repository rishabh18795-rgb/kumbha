About us

A gateway to a lighter, freer way of living

Kumbha Gateway focuses on bringing the optimal quality to the lifestyle of an individual who aspires to live free from worries, negativity and stress.

We offer courses, retreats and programs that power up a person — especially women — to live a vibrant, happy life, enjoying each moment and crafting a life of their wildest dreams.

Services include

Ten ways into a steadier, lighter life

Every session is offered individually or as part of a longer retreat or program, in Belagavi or online.

Energy & Yoga Practices

Breath · Movement · Prana

Kundalini BreathFlow

A rhythmic breathing meditation that activates dormant life-force energy at the base of the spine, clearing emotional blocks from the nervous system and improving lung capacity.

Chakra Yoga

Targeted movement and simple asanas that work through your seven energy centres, building the strength and clarity to sustain your most grounded, confident self.

Mudra Meditations

Symbolic hand gestures used to direct the flow of prana with precision, balancing the panch-mahabhutas — the five elements — within the body.

Retreats & Inner Work

Healing · Relationships · Growth

Tantra Retreats for Women & Couples

Retreats built on presence and conscious intimacy, dissolving defensive emotional armour to build safety, connection and trust — with yourself and with a partner.

Inner Child Healing

A gentle process of revisiting the younger versions of yourself to resolve old emotional scripts inherited from family, freeing you to take bolder risks in life today.

Personality Transformation Courses

A guided process of letting go of outdated survival habits and consciously shaping a version of yourself aligned with your presence, confidence and ambitions.

Fitness

Dance · Movement · Release

Zumba & Mat Pilates

High-energy Latin and international dance-fitness paired with mat Pilates — a workout that feels like a dance party while it builds strength and releases tension.

Rashmi certified in Zumba, 2018

Mindset, Life & Wealth

Clarity · Purpose · Abundance

Reality Creation Techniques

Working with thoughts, emotions, words and actions to consciously design the life you want, rather than defaulting to old patterns.

Business & Money Enhancer Courses

Sessions that clear old money stories and blocks around financial expansion, helping you build a steadier relationship with wealth in business and daily life.
